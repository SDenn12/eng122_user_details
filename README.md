### Python Operators
###
#### Two types of Operators
##### Arithmetic Operators
- `+ - * /`
- `+` adds two operands (variables)
- `-` subtracts
- `*` multiply
- `/` divide
- `**` exponent
- `%` returns remainder
##### Comparison Operators 
- `>` greater than
- `<` less than
- `==` is equal to
- `!=` not equal to
- `>=` greater than or equal to
- `<=` less than or equal to
